Urban
CLIENT

Urban

YEAR

2020

PROJECT

UX | UI | Front-End

TEAM

Ellen Creatives

Summary

To solve a problem

Our task was to create a website or an app that solves a problem.

In the creative process, everyone in the team came up with a few ideas of projects, and in the end we settled for: "a cityguide of Stockholm, for locals by locals" and "a travel planner tailored to the user's budget".

The concept is created for our persona Urban Urban, A curious urban person who wants to change the every day routines against something new and exciting within culture, events, food and night life.

Once settled for the idea, target group and evaluation of the research, it was time to conceptualise. We started the process by identifying the functions we wanted to include in the app. From that we could build our sitemap, and then start to sketch the wireframes on paper.

During our wireframe work, we tested the App's function and structure with a small group of test persons. All where asked to complete a certain task. From each test, issues or concerns were identified and improvements were implemented.

Our building blocks

Case output

Urban - For locals, by locals

In the end we went for a more basic app than what we initially thought, with the concept turning out to be something between Tripadvisor, Instagram and ThatsUp but with the main important feature and USP: Urban is the app for locals, by locals.

Everyone we presented the app to, really liked the idea and expressed that this was a service that they would actually use to explore their own city.

And the saga continues

A straight-to-the-point landing page filled with the beautiful elements and icons from the App.

The landing page has one sole mission; Get the users to download the App.
