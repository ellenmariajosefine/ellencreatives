Summary

To solve a problem

Our task was to create a website or an app that solves a problem.

In the creative process, everyone in the team came up with a few ideas of projects, and in the end we settled for: "a cityguide of Stockholm, for locals by locals" and "a travel planner tailored to the user's budget".

The concept is created for our persona Urban Urban, A curious urban person who wants to change the every day routines against something new and exciting within culture, events, food and night life.
